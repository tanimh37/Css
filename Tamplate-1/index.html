Details about Dhaka to Cox’s Bazar Flight Cox’s Bazar is one of the places of interest in Bangladesh. The longest beach in the world is located in Cox’s Bazar. Its total length is 155 km. Cox’s Bazar was once known as Panya and its ancient name was Palang. Every year a huge number of domestic and foreign tourists gather to enjoy the beauty of this long beach. Tourists from outside Bangladesh have to come to Cox’s Bazar via Dhaka and Chittagong.
